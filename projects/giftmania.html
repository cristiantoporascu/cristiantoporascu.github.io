During the first term of my second year as a Games Technology student at Coventry University, I created a game which helped I particisipated with to a competition in Brasil at Facens University(Sorocaba). The game was made in Unity and most of the assets were made in 3dsmax.

The things I learned from that experience were how different from other cultures see games, and how other univesirties are teaching their students the Sotfware Development knowledge.

I might want to change things in the game but not because I think something went wrong during the process of development but because the game might catch with the people and I according with people reactions I can go further with its development. For that I have found some platforms which allow to new developers to have a decent start for their future into game development for PC games. Those are Agame.com, GamesGames.com or itch.io.
