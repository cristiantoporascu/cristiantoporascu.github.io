I am proud to share our fun sidescroller game and the 1st place winner of CUCC 24 Hour Hackathon 2017: TrumpToupee https://lnkd.in/gScneTp It was a blast to make and I hope that it is a blast for everyone to play! The team was made with 3*1st year Games Tech students and 3*3rd year Games Tech students. Created with: Ogrezeanu Gabriel Razvan, Iveta Stripeikaite, Andrew Finlay, Lewis Jordan, Mozamel Rasouli.

My contribution was the development of the connection thorugh AWS in order to be able to host our network system for the leaderboard which I also developed. I helped also with the main Unity Team Services setup and the startup of the project for the slide scrolling and paralaxing functionality.

Our insperiation came from original idea was to take a viral video at the moment and take advantage of the opportunity. After some research we realised how a good idea this is and challanged ourself with completing a game on the current viral theme Donald Trump.

The game is a sidescroller game, here the character (Trump) runns and collects tupee's in order to get luscious locks
